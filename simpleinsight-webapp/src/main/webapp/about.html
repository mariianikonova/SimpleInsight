What is SimpleInsight?


Let's get some facts straight:

  • This is not a project management tool. It may seem that way, but it's not. It's meant to manage your processes and flow, not to manage your entire project.What's a resource anyway?
  • It doesn't use complicated concepts, cryptographic acronyms and exotic catchy terms to justify why it's good for you - it lets you judge for yourself

Back to business:

SimpleInsight is an online task management system that helps you manage your activities and processes.
It works flawlessly for personal task management but shines when you use it to manage projects that require team collaboration or input from multiple participants.

The concept is geared toward individual and group flow, including everything from simple activities to orchestrated workflows, while providing:

  • A simple and clear learning path
  • Repetitive evaluation and feedback loops
  • Formal process description and outcome declaration
  • Providing a high degree of interaction and collaboration to help you get the job done
  • A dynamic and personal experience for pragmatic, outcome-oriented work

We love talking about these things, so if you want to talk, just contact us.
