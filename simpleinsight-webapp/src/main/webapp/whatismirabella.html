What is Mirabella?


Let's get some facts straight:

  • Mirabella is not an LMS - it's an online learning support system built for people
  • Mirabella does not manage your courses - you do, the system just helps
  • Mirabella does not use complicated concepts, cryptographic acronyms and exotic catchy terms to justify why it's good for you - it lets you judge for yourself

Back to business:

Mirabella is an online learning support system that helps you learn, manage your courses, manage the learning activities of others and create meaningful and easy to use educational content. That just means that we try to support learning in any way we can, helping learners, trainers and training managers as best we can.

Mirabella is also an online course provider, featuring courses built in-house, by partners or even by trainers that want to get access to a wider audience.

The concept is geared toward individual learner backgrounds, interests and objectives, focusing on:

  • A simple and clear learning path
  • Repetitive self-evaluation and feedback
  • Formal testing and certification paths
  • Providing a high degree of interaction and collaboration that supports the learning process
  • A dynamic and personal experience for continuous learning

We love talking about these things, so if you want to talk, just contact us.
